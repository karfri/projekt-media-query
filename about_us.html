Meet the Team

Karin Frinell

Co-Founder and Marketing Strategist

Goal-oriented professional and creative mind of our company. After graduation in Marketing and Consumption at the University of Gothenburg Karin decided to travel and explore different cultures and the food habits. She continued to study Health Science in The University of Sydney for a two years. So Karin is not only a expert in healthy diet, her positon of marketing strategist involves a variety of tasks, including designing digital marketing, campaigns and performing market research.

Anton Stjernquist

Co-Funder and Procurement Specialist

With an eye for detail and order, excellent math and computer skills Anton is an important part of our team. Focused on a career in management Anton studied first Bachelor at Gothenburg School of Business, Economics and Law. To continue with Master in Supply Chain and Procurement Management at MIP Politecnico di Milano School of Management was a easy choice for Anton. He speaks fluently italian and he improved his pizza making skills to master level!

Anna Faixova

Co-Founder and Customer Service Manager

A problem solver with leadership and communication skills. How to motivate people, how to lead a team were always Annas big interests. She gruaduated in Bachelor of Business, Economics and Management in Slovakia Anna took a chance to study in Sweden, at Gothenburg School of Business, Economics and Law, where she met Karin and Anton. The rest is history, La Rango was born...
